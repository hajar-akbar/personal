Assoc. Prof. Dr. H. Nur Ihsan Hl, M.Hum

Affiliation

Rector of Universitas Sembilanbelas November Kolaka

Biography

H. Nur Ihsan Hl (born on Bulukumba 11 February 1968 ) is a Rector of Universitas Sembilanbelas November Kolaka at this time. He is an Associate Professor on Indonesian Language & Literature Education. Nur Ihsan was graduated from Indonesian language Education at Halu Oleo University, Kendari, Indonesia in 1991, than continued his Magister Program at Hasanuddin University (UNHAS) in 2003 and Doctoral Program at Universitas Negeri Jakarta (UNJ) in 2012 on Linguistics Study Program. H. Nur Ihsan Hl is also active as a researcher in the fields of linguistics, Indonesian Education, and Learning Technology studies with publications that have been published in various Reputated International and Accredited National journals. He is also active in various scientific meeting forums to disseminate research results obtained from external grants from the Ministry and Internal Research Institutions. Dr. H. Nur Ihsan HL., M,Hum. Start his carier be a vice rector of Universitas Sembilanbelas November Kolaka (USN Kolaka) in 2006-2008. In 2015 he lead the research centre (LPPM-PMP) USN Kolaka, and be a Head of Academic and Students Affairs USN Kolaka in 2017. He continued his carier as a dean of Faculty of Education and Teacher Training USN Kolaka in 2019-2022. Than now he is a Rektor of USN Kolaka in 2022-2026.
