Prof. Dr. H. Syahrul Yasin Limpo, S.H., M.Si., M.H

Syahrul Yasin Limpo was Born in Makassar, March 16th 1955. Syahrul is an Indonesian politician, who has served as Minister of Agriculture in Joko Widodo's Onward Cabinet since 23 October 2019. He was governor of South Sulawesi from 2008 to 2018

Syahrul was graduated college degree from Faculty of Law, University of Hasanuddin (1983) and got postgraduate (2004) then doctoral degree (2008) from University of Hasanuddin.

In 2017, after a meeting with Imam Muhammad ibn Saud Islamic University rector Sulaiman ibn Abdullah Abalkhail, Syahrul helped facilitate the opening of a Saudi-funded university in South Sulawesi.

In 2017, Syahrul oversaw a controversial project in the province to create five artificial islands in order to increase public space at Makassar. Opposition came from environmental organizations which filed a lawsuit and local fishermen who attempted to physically block the construction, claiming the project would deplete fish stocks.
